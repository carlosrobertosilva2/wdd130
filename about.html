Our History

Founded in 1985 in Colorado, we started with a single raft and a passion for adventure. Today, we're the leading rafting company in the Rocky Mountains, with over 50 certified guides.

We're committed to sustainable tourism and safety. All trips meet international rafting standards, and we've had zero major accidents since inception.
